Engineering note: This is a planning and visualization tool, not a motion controller. A straight commanded path still depends on simultaneous axis start, compatible controller interpolation, servo response, following-error limits, mechanics, and vendor-specific motion behavior. Verify the resulting settings on the actual machine at a safe reduced speed.
